Our story

Built in Paradise,
CA — literally.

NormandinTECH is a solo software company founded by Seth Normandin in Paradise, California — the town destroyed by the 2018 Camp Fire, rebuilt by the people who stayed.

Every product is built and shipped independently, using AI as a development and business partner. The goal is simple: build software that solves real problems, price it fairly, and ship it fast.

GridIQ was built here because Paradise knows what happens when the grid fails. The real estate suite was built because agents, landlords, and brokers deserve better tools than generic AI with no domain knowledge.

No VC. No team. No excuses. Just shipping.
