The problems every cannabis operator knows too well

01
Compliance changes overnight
State regs shift constantly. Florida's 64ER25-6, New York's billboard ban, FCC TCPA updates — operators learn about changes after they're already in violation.
02
Ad platforms are hostile territory
Instagram, Google, and Facebook restrict cannabis advertising. Shadow bans, account flags, and takedowns cost operators visibility they can't buy back.
03
Inventory decisions are gut-feel
Most dispensaries know what sold last week but not why. Overstock kills margins. Stockouts kill loyalty. Neither needs to happen with real AI forecasting.
04
Debt wave hitting in 2026
A major wave of cannabis debt is maturing. Operators need to squeeze more profit from existing operations — without massive new capital investment.
